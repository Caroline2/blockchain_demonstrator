How to use the app?

May 21, 2021

There is a lot of talk about cryptocurrencies these days - perhaps there is no person who has not heard of one. However, many such people do not realize that the use of blockchain extends to other industries and often do not even understand the technology behind the cryptocurrencies - blockchain. This makes people confused, distrustful and so they don't know what to think about it all. They can also fall victim to fraudsters in the area. This application should help users to understand at least the basic principles of blockchain technology to get some basic knowledge in order to be objective and able to avoid such uncomfortable situations.

The app was created by a high school student Karolina Podivinska as a practical part of her SOC work. It should be able to bring people closer to how blockchain technology works. For these purposes, the application combines two types of education - a theoretical summary of functionality and the demonstrator itself, where each user can experience first-hand what it is like to work with blockchain, send an illustration transaction, etc.

The main purpose of the app is to show how blockchain technology works, both both in basic theoretical and practical form.


The source codes are freely available on github so that every user can view, download and try to run them or just be inspired by them to create his/her own application.

The whole SOC work can be read here here, unfortunately only in Czech. However, there is also a YouTube video summarizing the work available at this link, which has English subtitles. In the short video Karolina speaks about some technological properties of blockchain, but also shows the app and talks about her motivation, too.
